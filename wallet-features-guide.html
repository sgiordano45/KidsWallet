The Magic of Compound Interest
How your money grows even while you sleep

Compound interest is when you earn interest not just on your original savings, but also on the interest you've already earned. It's like a snowball rolling downhill ‚Äî it keeps getting bigger!

üìà Example: $100 with 5% monthly interest

Month 1 Interest (+5%) + $5.00 ‚Üí $105.00
Month 2 Interest (+5%) + $5.25 ‚Üí $110.25
Month 3 Interest (+5%) + $5.51 ‚Üí $115.76

Getting Started
